Sternberg Working Memory Test

On the following trials,you will be see a sequence of patterns presented one at a time printed in white.
Immediately following each sequence, you will see a final pattern. Your task is to decide whether or not the final pattern appeared in the preceding sequence.

If the final pattern appeared in the sequence, press the left('A') key.
If the final pattern was NOT in the sequence, press the right('D') key.

Please enter your name and click the 'Start' button to begin.
